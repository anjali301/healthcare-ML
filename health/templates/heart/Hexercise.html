Diet plan for Heart Disease

Food to eat:
  • Fruits and Vegetables
  • Low-fat dairy products
  • Skinless poultry
  • Nuts, beans, and legumes
  • Fish , eggs(you can eat up to six per week)
  • plant-based oils, such as olive oil
Food to avoid:
  • Junk food
  • Alcohol
  • Hydrogenated vegetable oils
  • Processed frozen meals
  • Red meat
  • Condiments such as mayonnaise, ketchup, and packaged dressing
For more information visit the following websites:
  • Healthline
  • MedicalNewsToday

Exercise to follow

Be fit to fight:

  • Brisk walking
  • Running
  • Swimming
  • Playing tennis
  • Working out with free weights
  • Stretching
  • Balance exercise
  • Aerobic exercise
  • Strength training
  • Jumping rope

Follow the exercises & enjoy the results :)
