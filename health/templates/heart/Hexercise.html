Diet plan for Heart Disease

Food to eat:
  • Fruits and Vegetables
  • Low-fat dairy products
  • Skinless poultry
  • Nuts, beans, and legumes
  • Fish , eggs(you can eat up to six per week)
  • plant-based oils, such as olive oil
Food to avoid:
  • Junk food
  • Alcohol
  • Hydrogenated vegetable oils
  • Processed frozen meals
  • Red meat
  • Condiments such as mayonnaise, ketchup, and packaged dressing
For more information visit the following websites:
  • Healthline
  • MedicalNewsToday

Exercise to follow

The symptoms of Heart disease include:

  • Chest pain
  • Weakness
  • Shortness of breath
  • Coldness in your legs or arms
  • Pain in the neck, jaw, throat
  • High blood pressure
  • Nausea and cold sweats
  • Stomach pain
  • Pain in upper abdomen or back
  • Irregular heartbeat

Do you feel like you are having more than three or four symptoms? Go to our online tool to predict whether there is a chance of you having the heart disease.
