Diet plan for Liver Disease

Food to eat:
  • Fruits and Vegetables
  • Daily cup of coffee
  • Oatmeal or other high fiber foods
  • Green leafy vegetales and beans
  • Nuts, beans, and legumes
  • Fish
  • Turmeric, sunflower seeds as antioxidants
Food to avoid:
  • Fatty Food
  • Food with excess starch
  • Sugary Foods
  • Excess Salts
  • Alcohol
For more information visit the following websites:
  • Healthline
  • MedicalNewsToday
  • LiverFoundation

Exercise to follow

The symptoms of Heart disease include:

  • Chest pain
  • Weakness
  • Shortness of breath
  • Coldness in your legs or arms
  • Pain in the neck, jaw, throat
  • High blood pressure
  • Nausea and cold sweats
  • Stomach pain
  • Pain in upper abdomen or back
  • Irregular heartbeat

Do you feel like you are having more than three or four symptoms? Go to our online tool to predict whether there is a chance of you having the heart disease.
