Liver Disease

Your liver does a lot of things that keep you healthy. It turns nutrients into chemicals your body needs. It filters out poisons. It helps turn food into energy. So when your liver doesn’t work well, that can affect your whole body. Different things can cause serious liver conditions. You’ll want to know about the top causes. Alcohol-Related Liver Disease is caused by damage to the liver from years of excessive drinking. Years of alcohol abuse can cause the liver to become inflamed and swollen. This damage can also cause scarring known as cirrhosis. Cirrhosis is the final stage of liver disease.

Symptoms of Liver Disease

The symptoms of Liver disease include:

  • Nausea
  • Jaundice
  • Fatigue
  • Abdominal Discomfort
  • Increased thirst
  • Drastic skin colour change
  • Fainting
  • Unusual agitation
  • Mood swings
  • Bleeding gums

Do you feel like you are having more than three or four symptoms? Go to our online tool to predict whether there is a chance of you having the liver disease.
