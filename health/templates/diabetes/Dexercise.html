Food to eat:
  • Vegetables:

  • Berries

Food to avoid:
  • Sugar-sweetened beverages
  • Trans fats
  • White bread, rice, and pasta
  • Flavored processed food
  • Sweetened breakfast cereals
  • Honey, agave nectar, and maple syrup
  • Dried fruit
  • Fried food
  • Canned vegetables with lots of added sodium
  • Jam & jelly
  • Regular cheeses
  • Poultry with skin
  • Ice cream
  • Energy Drinks
